What It Is

A private, turn-based word duel.

Jotto is a head-to-head guessing game. Each player chooses a five-letter secret word with no repeated letters, then takes turns guessing the other player's word.

After every guess, you see how many letters you got right, but not where they belong. The rest is deduction.

How It Works

  1. Create a private room or join a public match.
  2. Pick a five-letter secret word with no duplicate letters.
  3. Take turns guessing and narrowing the possibilities.
  4. Win by guessing the exact word first.

Text Alerts

Optional turn notifications by SMS.

After logging in, players can open Notification Settings and choose to receive a text when it becomes their turn in a Jotto game.

By entering a phone number and requesting a verification code, the user agrees to receive turn-based text alerts for gameplay. Message frequency varies by game activity. Message and data rates may apply. Consent is not required to use Jotto. Players can remove text notifications at any time from Notification Settings. Reply STOP to opt out and HELP for help.
