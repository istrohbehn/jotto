Privacy Policy

Public Site Terms of Service Play Jotto

Jotto stores account information, room data, guesses, and game history needed to operate the service.

If a player enables text alerts, Jotto stores the phone number, verification status, and alert preference needed to send turn notifications.

Phone numbers are used only for account verification and turn-based gameplay alerts. They are not sold to third parties.

Players can remove text notifications from Notification Settings. Removing notifications disables future gameplay texts.
